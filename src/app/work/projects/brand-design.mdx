## Overview

RoyalWine is a concept brand for a winery selling collection wines. The goal was to create an online presence that conveys elegance and exclusivity: a refined UI/UX that highlights elite products, tells the story of the winery and builds trust with collectors.

## Process

This is the most extensive project in my portfolio, following a complete design process documented in Figma: strategy (context, roadmap and sitemap), visual moodboard, brand identity (color exploration, typography and design direction), wireframes and UI concepts, the final UI design for every page of the store, a styleguide, and motion design explorations.
