## Overview

Velvet Visage is a beauty and makeup studio based in Chișinău. The project covers the full visual presence of the brand: a landing page, logo, business cards and a set of promotional banners, all built around a soft, feminine palette of rose, plum and gold.

## Landing page

A single-page website presenting the studio's services, portfolio, testimonials and contact, designed to feel elegant and welcoming while guiding visitors toward booking.
